# AFYA BAND

An affordable wristband that continuously monitors your blood pressure and heart rate, providing real-time health insights to help prevent serious complications.

## Why Afya band?

### The Problem
i- Urban residents struggle to balance demanding work schedules and stress
i- Many remain unaware of their blood pressure until complications arise
i- Late medical interventions lead to higher healthcare costs

### Our Solution
i- Affordable wristband for continuous health monitoring
i- Real-time blood pressure and heart rate tracking
i- Weekly health statistics and expert tips
i- Early warnings enable preventive action and timely medical advice
